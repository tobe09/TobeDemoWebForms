Chicken Wings Recipe


Ingredients
  • Chicken Wings
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Pepper

Preparation

Preparation Time:   30 minutes
Cook Time:             1 hour and 30 minutes
Total Time:             2 hours

Directions

  1. Boil for two hours.
  2. Fry for six hours
  3. Serve with rice
